BODY MASS INDEX CALCULATOR

A BMI Calculator is a tool that helps individuals assess their body weight in relation to their height. It calculates the Body Mass Index (BMI) using a simple formula: BMI = weight (kg) / height (m)^2. The resulting value categorizes individuals into different weight ranges, such as underweight, normal weight, overweight, and obesity. This information can be useful for monitoring health and making lifestyle adjustments.
